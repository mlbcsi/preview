Welcome to MLBCSI

RESPECT, LEADERSHIP, and EXCELLENCE

We respect everyone regardless of differences by treating them with courtesy, consideration, and kindness and creating an inclusive and supportive environment where students, educators, and staff feel valued and safe. We empower individuals to take initiative, demonstrate responsibility, and contribute positively inside and outside the school community. We value excellence in achieving the highest standards of quality, performance, and continuous improvement in the pursuit of one's full potential.

Buwan ng Wika: Hearts Ablaze Toward Culture

Daniela Nicole R. Sabanpan

“Buhay pa ba ang alab sa puso mo?” A touching inquiry from the movie GomBurZa, directed by Pepe Diokno, asked. This thought-provoking question challenges the hearts of the Filipinos. It pushes us to question how close we are to the tapestry of our heritage. The first event of the school year, Buwan ng Wika, reminds us of our culture and identity.

Buwan Ng Wika: Igniting Patriotism

Daniela Nicole R. Sabanpan

On August 31, 2023, Matilda L. Bradford Christian School Inc. celebrated Buwan ng Wika with the participation of students, parents, and faculty. ⁤⁤From the Lakan and Lakambini pageant to the Sabayang Pagbikas competition, the festivity encourages the preservation of Filipino heritage.

Buwan ng Wika: The Passion of the Filipino

Shean Eli T. Ecoy

The Buwan ng Wika is a special time in all of the Philippines where everyone wears the traditional attire of the Philippines. It is a month to celebrate our culture. A month to appreciate ourselves and our language, Filipino. We hold this every year to commemorate our ancestors, and thank them for their service.
