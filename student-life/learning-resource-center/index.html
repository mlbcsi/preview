Learning Resource Center (LRC)

The Matilda L. Bradford Christian School, Inc. Learning Resource Center (LRC) is an integral part of the School and contributes to the realization of its objectives by providing and making available materials and services needed for the students formation and education.

LRC provides services mainly to MLBCSI students, faculty, administration and the school staff.

Vision/Mission Statement

The LRC aims to promote a wholistic formation of the students as well as the faculty and the whole community through developing enthusiasm and passion for reading by providing a rich and relevant collection of reading and audio visual materials to meet the recreational, cultural, spiritual, emotional, and academic needs of the community.
