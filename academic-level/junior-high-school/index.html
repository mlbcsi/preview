Guidance

The Guidance Services believe in the Confucian saying give a man a fish and he eats for a day, teach a man how to fish and he eats for a lifetime. Your Guidance Center helps you become self reliant, altruistic, and transcendental individuals through counseling, interventions, group/peer facilitations, mental health activities, and individual inventory service. These services are aimed at helping the individual understand himself and his world better.

To learn more about the Guidance Services, click here to go to our Guidance Services page under Student Life > Student Formation.
